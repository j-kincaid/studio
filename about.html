About

I am best known for my compositions of surreal scenery, nature, and abstract images. I am currently a resident artist at the Charlotte Street Foundation Studios in Kansas City, Missouri. My work has been acquired by the Nerman Museum of Contemporary Art, Emprise Bank, and numerous private collections.

This interview by Julie Denesha for Arts Upload features a detailed description of my process and artwork.
